Slyp
09/17/2018
I'm french canadian and this dessert was made at least every other week, because of its simplicity and low cost (she used a mix of brown sugar and water instead of maple sirup and did not use cream or other dairy). I compared it to my mom's and hers was better. Truth be told, the cake part was about the same, but she would bake it in a square glass dish (8in x 8in), which allowed all the liquid to be poured onto, which turned this moistened cake into a pudding. I highly recommend the use of a dish allowing all the cream/sirup to be poured and baked with the cake.
