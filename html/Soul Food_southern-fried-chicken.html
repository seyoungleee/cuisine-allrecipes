Dave Svoboda
01/09/2020
This (no egg) is how my mom made fried chicken when I was a kid. She'd NOT wash the chicken, leaving the sticky exterior from the package in the fridge, then just dredge in flour. Then she'd put all the pieces on a rack and let them soak up the flour for 20 minutes or so, then dredge again. Fry in a quarter inch of veg oil, first one side on high heat, then flip all the pieces, turn the heat down to low medium, and put the lid on, and cook for 20 minutes. Then make gravy with the remaining oil and fond.
