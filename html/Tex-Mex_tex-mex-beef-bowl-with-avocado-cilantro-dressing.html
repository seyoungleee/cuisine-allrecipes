Mumkatt
08/24/2018
i made substitutions due to dietary (non-dialysis Kidney and Diabetes friendly) and needs like swapping half the black beans for corn and half the tomato for julienne'd roasted red peppers. And swapping out a half'n'half combo of Havarti and Cheddar for the Cotija, which is not easy to find in Canada and is also much higher in Sodium. I did not serve any carbohydrates other than the rice (NO Tortillas or Tortilla chips), also dietary. Didn't miss them at all. Also I served my hubbies with Avocado slices on the plate as he's not fond of Guacamole, which is what the dressing basically is. He simply used Salsa as a dressing. I found this meal to be enough for three or even 4 people. It was spicy with the Jalapenos but was otherwise a little bland. Next time I am going to double the Steak spice and add some to the Guac. or or just to some cilantro sour cream. BUT I did the steak exactly as written and it was OUTSTANDING!PS, the left overs the next day made a FANTASTIC WRAP on Tomato tortilla!
