VICTORIA here, I shared the technique. I've read all the reviews and will add some tips that might help. I've substituted the oil, salt, and vinegar with no change ro the end product.Rinse, dry, then put the chicken breast in the freezer for about 15 minutes or so. It firms it up so you can cut it into the thin strips we all love in our take-out. After the 1 MINUTE in water, drain and dry on paper towels. Wet proteins will steam but never brown. Add the chicken to an oiled high temp at the last moments before you make the sauce. This technique works well with beef also. I hope this helps! There are several velveting techniques online. If this one isn't for you, look them up. But it's never failed for me.
