cltncook
06/30/2017
These ribs were fabulous! Made with 3 pounds of ribs and did everything else as written. I did incorporate a few suggestions though. Added 1/2 c. water to braising pot. Added 1/4 c. braising liquid to sauce. Added 1/4 tsp. red pepper flakes to sauce. Everything was so easy and delicious. Had just the right amount of sauce--only 1 Tbs. left when they were done. While I was waiting for the oven to preheat, I just set the ribs, meat side down, in the pan of sauce and let them soak (I also basted several times) while the oven heated. Then, basted every 8 minutes for a total of 32 minutes cooking time in the oven. Beautiful mahogany color, tender yet still with a little chew. For us, this was dinner for two; it was a half a slab each and we thoroughly enjoyed. Three pounds probably amounts to 4 regular servings. I'll be sharing this recipe with lots of family and friends. They're gonna love it! Oh, yeah. I saved the braising liquid and onions and I think I'll make a pot of rice with it. We'll see. Too good to toss. Don't forget to line your pan with foil--you'll be sorry if you don't.
