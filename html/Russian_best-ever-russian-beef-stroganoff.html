Overall this was a good recipe. Loved making the gravy for it! I’m giving 3 stars instead of 5 because I found it slightly bland without adding extra to it, and had to wing it a little after tasting it. I made a few changes. I used ground beef cooked with a little flour, Salt, and pepper. I added another 1/4 tsp of salt at the end, another 1/4 cup beef broth to thin it a little more, 6 cloves of garlic in total (we love garlic) and, another 1/2 tsp worcestershire. I did 2 tbsp Sherry and tbsp off the Worcestershire to start instead of all sherry. Family’s general consensus is that they’re uncertain of the tangy flavor, but it’s okay. I liked it enough to eat it again, but it wasn’t my favorite.
