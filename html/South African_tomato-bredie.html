I did adapt this but the base is fabulous! I used small fresh cherry tomatoes, tri-tip steak since I had no lamb, and a combination of red garnet yams and one Russett potato. I also used some broth I made from a goat shank bone instead of a bouillon cube. Since I'm gluten-free, I used Arrowroot instead of flour to dredge the meat and added Harissa seasoning, three cloves of garlic, and one poblano chile chopped for extra spice. It simmered down beautifully making a rich, thick gravy and very tender meat. I will definitely make this again! Thank you. I forgot to take a picture.
