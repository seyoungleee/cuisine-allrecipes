12/25/2021
I have made these a few times now and they always come out great. I add baking powder and salt since I don’t use self rising flour. One and a half teaspoons of baking powder and a quarter teaspoon of salt per cup of flour. Sprinkle a little flour over the dough before you wrap it in plastic and when you open it up the next morning you can pat the dough out on the plastic wrap and cut it, transferring to the baking sheet with no mess. Light and fluffy biscuits!
